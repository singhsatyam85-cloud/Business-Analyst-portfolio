# Stakeholder Register

## Project Name
ABC School Web and Mobile Application

## Prepared By
Shiv Satyam Singh, Business Analyst

## Sprint
Sprint 0: Discovery and Setup

| Stakeholder / Role | Organisation | Project Role | Interest in Project | Priority | Communication Method |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| School Owner | ABC School | Business Sponsor | Wants the application to improve school operations | High | Discovery meeting / approval meeting |
| Principal | ABC School | Product Owner | Provides business priorities and approves requirements | High | Meetings / Confluence review |
| Admin Staff | ABC School | Key User | Manages student data, teacher data and attendance reports | High | Requirement meeting / UAT session |
| Teacher Representative | ABC School | Key User | Uses the mobile app to mark and submit attendance | High | Requirement meeting / UAT session |
| Scrum Master | Delivery Team | Facilitator | Manages Agile ceremonies and removes process confusion | Medium | Jira / team meetings |
| Business Analyst | Delivery Team | Requirement Owner | Documents requirements, user stories and acceptance criteria | High | Jira / Confluence / meetings |
| Developer | Delivery Team | Technical Delivery | Builds web and mobile application features | Medium | Jira / sprint ceremonies |
| QA Tester | Delivery Team | Testing | Reviews test scenarios and validates functionality | Medium | Jira / test review meeting |
| DevOps Engineer | Delivery Team | Deployment Support | Supports environment setup and release activities | Low | Jira / release meeting |

## BA Notes
The high-priority stakeholders for discovery are the Principal, Admin Staff and Teacher Representative because they define the core business rules and user journeys.
