Our Story

The Foundation

From a Local Garage to a National Hub

Founded in the early 2000s, Auto Parts SME began as a small specialist sourcing unit for local independent workshops. We identified a critical gap in the market: the lack of high-quality, verified OEM-alternative parts that trade professionals could trust.

By 2012, we had transitioned into a full-scale distribution center, leveraging direct relationships with manufacturers to cut out the middleman and deliver cost-savings without compromising on safety or performance.
