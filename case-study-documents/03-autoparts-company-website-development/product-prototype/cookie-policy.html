Introduction

At Auto Parts SME, we prioritize technical precision and professional reliability. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website. In the automotive trade, efficiency is paramount. Cookies help us ensure that our catalog, search tools, and enquiry forms function with the "first-time-right" accuracy you expect from a professional partner.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used by website owners in order to make their websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

First-Party Cookies

Cookies set by the website owner (Auto Parts SME). These are essential for core functions like the quote request system.

Third-Party Cookies

Cookies set by parties other than the website owner, used for analytics, advertising, and interactive features.
