Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping & Logistics Support

Can I track my part in real-time? expand_more
Yes. Once your part leaves our central hub, you will receive a tracking ID via SMS and email. For trade heavy-freight, we provide direct contact with the logistics dispatcher.
Do you ship internationally? expand_more
Currently, we focus on UK Mainland, Highlands, and Islands. For international trade enquiries, please contact our export desk directly for a custom freight quote.
What if a part is damaged in transit? expand_more
All parts are insured. If a package arrives damaged, please document the condition immediately and contact our claims department within 24 hours for a priority replacement.
Are surcharge components collected? expand_more
Yes. For items like alternators or turbos with a surcharge, we can arrange a 'core collection' service for trade partners. Private customers can return old cores via our standard return portal.
